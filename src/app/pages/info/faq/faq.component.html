FAQs

What are fees in transactions?

Fees are payment made to the volunteers who process transactions on a blockchain (miners).

How much minimum ZooBC I can send?

The minimum ZooBC you can send is 0.00000001 ZBC (1 ZooBit).

Where can I find my ZooBC address?

You can find your ZooBC address in Dashboard page (below account name) or you can find it in Receive page (mark with purple background)

What is the difference between balance and spendable balance?

Balance is how much zbc you have currently, regardless if you still have unconfirmed zbc.

Spendable balance is how much zbc you have minus unconfirmed zbc you have. So you can't send 5 ZBC if you have 3 ZBC in spendable balance, even though you have 10 ZBC in balance

I sent zbcs to the wrong ZooBC address. How can I get it back?

ZooBC transaction can't be reversed. If you send ZBC to the wrong address by mistake it cannot be recovered.

How to Properly Save & Back Up Your Wallet?

In order to make your wallet secure, write down your seed phrase in some paper and store it in a safe place. And never tell anyone your seed phrase or where you keep your seed phrase

Restoring ZooBC Wallet from seed phrase

You can restore your ZooBC wallet by following these steps:

  1. In login or home page. Click "Open wallet"
  2. In restore wallet page, type your seed phrase correctly
  3. Click "Open Wallet" button
  4. Set your new PIN
